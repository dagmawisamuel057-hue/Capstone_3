# Capstone 1: Maine Sales Territory Analysis

# Project Overview
This project analyzes sales performance for the Maine sales territory for EmporiUm, a fictional business that sells bookstore, technology, art supply, and merchandise products. The goal of this project is to use SQL and Excel to answer sales manager questions, identify trends, and make a recommendation for next quarter.

# Analysis Questions Answered
1. Total revenue for Maine and the date range covered by the data
2. Month-by-month revenue breakdown for Maine
3. Comparison of Maine revenue to the Northeast region
4. Number of transactions per month and average transaction size by product category
5. Ranking of Maine store performance
6. Recommendation for where to focus sales attention next quarter

# Key Findings
Maine generated $1,877,249.75 in total revenue from January 1, 2022 through December 31, 2025. The monthly revenue trend shows overall growth over time, with higher revenue levels in later periods. Compared to the full Northeast region, Maine contributes a smaller portion of total revenue, which suggests there is room for growth.

The store ranking showed that South Portland was the top-performing Maine store. Product and category analysis also showed that technology-related products were strong revenue drivers.

# Recommendation
Based on the analysis, the company should focus on increasing average transaction size in lower-performing Maine stores. Since transaction counts are relatively similar across stores, increasing the value of each purchase could improve overall revenue. Strategies could include promoting higher-value products, bundling products, and applying successful sales strategies from top-performing stores like South Portland.
